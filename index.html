Drag out the blue bar on the right of the screen to reveal the sidebar.
Bring nodes into the workspace by dragging any of the red boxes in the sidebar.
Start by dragging the one that says "result", then the one that says "PI".
Connect the two circles by dragging from one to the other.
The result node should now display the number 3.14159...
Disconnect any two circles by clicking either.
Circles found on the left side of a node are inputs, and the right side outputs.
Any input may connect to any output not found on the same node.
A circle may only be connected to one other circle at a time.
To change the value of the val node, click on its number.
